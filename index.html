Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How can I join the Youth Empowerment Cell?
You can join YEC by filling out the application form on our website or by contacting us directly. We welcome passionate individuals who want to make a difference in their communities.
What programs do you offer for youth?
We offer various programs including digital skills training, leadership development, community engagement initiatives, and health and safety awareness. Our programs are designed to empower youth with practical skills for the future.
Are your programs free of charge?
Most of our programs are offered free of charge to ensure accessibility for all youth. Some specialized training programs may have a nominal fee, but we offer scholarships for those who cannot afford it.
How can I volunteer with YEC?
We welcome volunteers who are passionate about youth empowerment. You can apply through our website or contact us directly to discuss volunteer opportunities that match your skills and interests.
